To Drink From The Sea of Gaza

This game addresses sensitive events and promotes peace and understanding.
"To Drink From The Sea of Gaza" is an anti-war game and a pacifist game that remembers the 7th of October massacre and calls for a ceasefire and the return of the Israeli hostages and an end to war.

Game Overview

You play as an Israeli hostage who was kidnapped to Gaza on the 7th of October. Having managed to escape your abductors, you must find your way back to Israel through Gaza.

Gameplay

Each day, you must make crucial decisions about which direction to take:

  • Choose between going North or South
  • Choose between going East or West

Outcomes

  • Successfully reaching Israeli forces leads to freedom
  • Being recaptured results in returning to captivity
This game is available in both Hebrew and English text.
