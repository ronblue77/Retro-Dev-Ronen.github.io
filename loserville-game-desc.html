Meet Roy (Ray of Sunshine)

A passionate GW-BASIC programmer living with mental illness, working at a grocery store, and finding solace in coding during weekends. Though some may judge him as a "loser," his story reveals the complexity and worth of every human life.

Part One: The Small Life

Experience Roy's daily struggles as he balances his protected job, programming passion, and life on social security. Navigate online relationships where his true value often goes unrecognized.

Part Two: Streets of Survival

After losing everything to hackers, follow Roy's journey through homelessness. Encounter both kindness and cruelty from strangers, facing the harsh realities of street life.

Part Three: Kingdom of Heaven

Now as Ray of Hope and Sunshine, explore the afterlife, confront past choices, and learn the power of forgiveness. Make the ultimate decision between peaceful rest or a second chance at life.
