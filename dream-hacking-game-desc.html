DREAM HACKING

Enter the Dream Terminal

DREAM HACKING is a unique QB45 text adventure with immersive graphics and haunting sound effects that explores the thin line between our dreams and reality. As a skilled dream hacker, you'll navigate the mysterious realm of the subconscious to uncover hidden truths about the nature of existence itself.

Use the powerful Dream Terminal to manipulate dream landscapes, extract information, and alter perceptions. But remember, the deeper you go into the dream world, the more you risk your own sanity.

Key Features

  • Immersive text-based gameplay combined with retro graphics
  • Atmospheric sound effects that enhance the dream experience
  • Multiple dream landscapes to explore and hack
  • A sanity meter that you must carefully manage
  • Multiple endings based on your choices and actions
  • Classic QB45 retro aesthetic with modern gameplay concepts
  • An original story exploring consciousness, reality, and perception

SANITY WARNING

Every action in the dream world affects your mental stability. Push too far, and you may never find your way back to reality. The dream terminal is a powerful tool, but it comes with risks. Use it wisely.
