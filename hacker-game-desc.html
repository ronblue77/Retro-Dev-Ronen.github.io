The Mission

Step into the digital underworld of the 1990s as a skilled hacker contracted by the mafia. Your target: a bank employee in her mid-30s. But as you delve deeper into her digital footprint, you uncover a web of adversity surrounding her life - and the dark reality that your employers have no intention of letting you walk away from this job.
