Mentally Ill

A story about challenges, resilience, and the journey to recovery

"Mentally Ill" is a game that sensitively explores the challenges of mental illness and the path to recovery. This interactive experience puts you in the shoes of a young person whose life takes an unexpected turn due to mental illness.

Your Journey

After being released from the hospital, you face a crucial decision: Will you see yourself as a victim of circumstance, or as someone who can still make choices and take responsibility for your future?

  • Navigate the challenges of returning to daily life
  • Work with family and friends who are also adjusting to changes
  • Make choices that affect your recovery journey
  • Build a new life while managing your condition
  • Find strength and hope in the face of adversity

    • Key Themes

    • Personal growth and resilience
    • Family support and understanding
    • The importance of hope and determination
    • Building a meaningful life despite challenges
    Remember: With commitment to hope and determination, you can rebuild your life and find pride in your journey.
